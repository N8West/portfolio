Who Am I??

I am a music lover and an avid live music enthusiast. I attend as many concerts and music festivals as I feasibly can. These experiences and the creativity demonstrated at these events have greatly influenced my own personal creativity and my work. It is this passion that drives me to provide the best possible development experience and ultimately the best web presence to all of my clients.

I served over 5 years as a Medic in the US Army National Guard. My experience and training taught me many universally desirable skills. The most important of which is the ability to pay extreme attention to detail, without losing sight of the 'big picture'. This is especially important in web development, just as it is treating casualties on the battlefield.

Starting or owning your own business is a great dream for many individuals. A key factor in the success or failure of any business is the image that it presents. These days having a beautiful, user friendly web page is vital to maintaining a positive company image. I can help those with small businesses achieve this by providing the best development experience available to your company or organization. I look forward to working with you!
